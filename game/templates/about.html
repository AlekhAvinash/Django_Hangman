Hangman!
Hangman is a word guessing game for two or more players. One player thinks of a word and represents it with dashes. The other player(s) guess letters one at a time. Correct guesses reveal the letter's position, while incorrect guesses lead to drawing a hangman figure. The game continues until the word is guessed or the hangman figure is completed. It's a fun way to improve vocabulary and spelling.
